# MediTech Auto-Injectors

A comprehensive RimWorld mod adding 12 craftable automatic injectors for emergency medical treatment, combat enhancement, and specialized utility purposes.

## Features

### 12 Different Auto-Injectors

**Medical/Emergency (6 types):**
- **Epinephrine** - Emergency cardiac treatment, restores consciousness
- **Morphine** - Powerful pain relief and sedation
- **Coagulant** - Rapid bleeding control
- **Detox** - Toxin and poison removal
- **Antibiotic** - Infection treatment and immunity boost
- **Regenerative** - Accelerated wound healing

**Combat/Performance (3 types):**
- **Combat Stimulant** - Enhanced combat performance
- **Berserker** - Extreme combat boost with severe side effects
- **Stealth Enhancer** - Improved stealth and movement

**Utility/Specialized (3 types):**
- **Psychic Dampener** - Psychic sensitivity reduction
- **Temperature Regulator** - Extreme temperature resistance
- **Radiation Shield** - Radiation protection

### Realistic Drug Mechanics

- **Tolerance System** - Repeated use builds tolerance, reducing effectiveness
- **Addiction & Withdrawal** - Each injector can cause addiction with withdrawal symptoms
- **Overdose System** - Using multiple injectors in short time causes dangerous overdose
- **Balanced Effects** - Each injector has specific buffs, debuffs, and side effects

### Research Progression

- **Basic Auto-Injectors** (1200 points) - Unlocks Morphine, Detox, Antibiotic, Temperature Regulator
- **Advanced Auto-Injectors** (2400 points) - Unlocks Epinephrine, Coagulant, Regenerative, Stealth Enhancer, Psychic Dampener, Radiation Shield
- **Military Stimulants** (3000 points) - Unlocks Combat Stimulant, Berserker

### Crafting & Administration

- **Crafting**: All injectors are craftable at the **Drug Lab** with varying skill requirements.
- **Surgery Administration**: Doctors can administer injectors to patients or prisoners via the **Operations/Surgery** tab.
- **Trader Availability**: Injectors can be purchased from Exotic, Combat, and Orbital traders.

## Installation

1. Download the mod
2. Extract to your RimWorld Mods folder
3. Enable in the mod manager
4. Start a new game or add to existing saves

## Compatibility

- **RimWorld Version:** 1.5 and 1.6
- **Combat Extended (CE):** Full support! Built-in patches for **Bulk** and **Weight** ensure injectors work perfectly with the CE inventory and loadout systems.
- **20+ Compatible Mods:** Designed to work alongside common mods like:
  - *Common Sense*, *Smart Medicine*, *Pharmacy*, *Pharmacist*, *Pick Up And Haul*, *RimHUD*, *Medical Tab*, *Better Pawn Control*, *Allow Tool*, *RunAndGun*, *Giddy-up!*, *Hospital*, *EPOE*, *RBSE*, *Vanilla Expanded*, *Dubs Bad Hygiene*, *Replimat*, *Simple Sidearms*, *Pocket Sand*, *Search and Destroy*, *Defensive Positions*.
- **Inventory Support:** All injectors are categorized for easy inventory carry and medical job prioritisation.
